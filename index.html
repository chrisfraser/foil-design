Hydrodynamic Foils

Understanding underwater foils well enough to design, compute, 3D-print, and water-test fishing depressors — towed wings that drive a lure deep while trolling.

The whole idea in one line
A depressor is an upside-down wing: it makes a force perpendicular to the flow — point that force down, and your lure dives.
